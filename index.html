About

The festival of Kali Puja is not an ancient one. Kali Puja was practically unknown before the 18th century, however a late 17th century devotional text Kalika mangalkavya â€“by Balram mentions an annual festival dedicated to Kali. It was introduced in Bengal during the 18th century, by King (Raja) Krishnachandra of Navadvipa. Kali Puja gained popularity in the 19th century, with Krishanachandra 's grandson Ishvarchandra and the Bengali elite; wealthy landowners began patronizing the festival on a grand scale. Along with Durga Puja, now - Kali Puja is the biggest goddess festival in Bengal.Garden reach kite club Puja Committee, are opulently celebrating the biggest festival of Bengal -Kali Puja , over the years. This club is one of the ancient organizations of Garden Reach, garden reach kite club Puja Committee was formed by some Local denizens. Since then, we are extending our effort to make this traditional Kali Puja celebration, the greatest of its kinds.

We have showcased some beautiful ' Traditions ' for the last few years which instigated our Puja to a different stratum. Since then the rate of footfall of the spectators has also increased alarmingly.Besides orchestration puja garden reach kite club Puja Committee is also actively engaged in various social welfare and cultural activities; the active sustenance by the increasing number of participants has helped us call it a great success.
